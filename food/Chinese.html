Chinese Cuisine Cooking

Ingredients:

  • Pork
  • Ginger
  • Spring onion
  • Cooking wine
  • Soy sauce
  • Rock sugar
  • Bay leaves
  • Star anise
  • Cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Cut the pork into pieces and blanch in boiling water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add ginger and spring onion, then fry the blanched pork until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Add cooking wine, soy sauce, rock sugar, bay leaves, star anise, and cinnamon, stir-fry evenly.
  4. Add water, simmer over low heat until the pork is tender and flavorful.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped spring onion before serving.

Cooking red-braised pork requires patience and attention to detail. We hope you enjoy this delicious Chinese dish!
