Small cravings! Let's make Rice

Here are the ingredients that you need to make Rice (for two people)

  • Rice = 1 regular bowl
  • Oil or ghee(saturated butter) = 2 serving spoon
  • cumin seeds = 2 teaspoon
  • salt = as per taste
  • onion = 1 medium size (chopped)
  • peas = 1 scoop
  • soya chunks = 1 small bowl (soak in water for 10 minutes)

Let's start the preparation.

  1. Take a big pan with the capacity of minimum 3 litres.
  2. Turn on the stove. Keep at high.
  3. Add oil or ghee in the pan until it becomes hot.
  4. Keep the stove at medium to high temprature.
  5. Add cumin seeds followed by chopped onion immediately.
  6. Add salt and saute it until onion turns golden brown
  7. Add peas, soya chunks, Rice and 2 bowls of water (use the bowl used to measure Rice)
  8. Cover the Pan and wait until the water is soaked. Check the rice after every 10 minutes during cooking.
  9. Once the water is completely soaked remove the pan from the stove. Turn off the stove. Wait for 10-15 minutes. Enjoy the meal. It can also be served with curd.
