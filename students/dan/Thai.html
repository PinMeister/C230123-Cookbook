Thai Cuisine - Red Curry Chicken

A delicious Thai dish, the recipe for red curry chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Red curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Onion
  • Green pepper
  • Chili
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Lime leaves
  • Lemongrass
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Preparation Steps:

Step 1:

Heat oil, add red curry paste, and stir-fry.

Step 2:

Add diced chicken, stir-fry until browned.

Step 3:

Add coconut milk, onion, green pepper, chili, garlic, ginger, lime leaves, and lemongrass.

Step 4:

Cook until chicken is tender, season with salt and pepper to taste.
